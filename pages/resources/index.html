Resources for Female Athletes

Mental Health & Support

  • Women's Sports Foundation - Advocating for women in sports through research, education, and leadership programs.
  • National Athletic Trainers' Association - Providing health and safety resources for athletes, including injury prevention and rehabilitation.
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) - Offering mental health resources, support groups, and advocacy for athletes and the general public.

Training & Nutrition

  • Girls Gone Strong - A community dedicated to strength training, nutrition, and wellness for women.
  • Team USA Nutrition - Expert-backed nutrition advice to enhance athletic performance and recovery.
  • Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics - Trusted nutritional guidance for optimizing health and performance.
